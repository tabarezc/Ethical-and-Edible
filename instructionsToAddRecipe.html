ADDING NEW RECIPES & INGREDIENTS

  • Step 0: Login using your user information.
  • Step 1: Enter a recipe name.
  • Step 2: Choose any ingredient from the List Of Ingredients and click Add button to add it to the Selected Ingredient box. You can add as many ingredients as you want. If you would like to remove any Selected Ingredients, select it and click Remove button.
  • Step 3: Click Add The Recipe button to submit the recipe.
